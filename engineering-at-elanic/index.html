6 months of Using React Native

24 January 2017

Here at Elanic, we use react-native (RN) to build our iOS application. When we started 7 months ago, RN was still a relatively new framework and the decision to use it wasn't very clear cut. Ultimately, it came down to our need to launch an iOS app quickly and the ability for the rest of the team to contribute code. After 4 months of learning and building, we released our iOS app in November. Today, I'd like to share some of the lessons we learned using RN to develop an Indian e-commerce application.
